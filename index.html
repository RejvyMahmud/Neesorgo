About Us

We capture moments, create stories, and celebrate imagination through the lens of creativity.

Our Vision

NEESORGO is more than just a photography society - we are a community of visual storytellers passionate about capturing the world through our lenses. Founded in 2018, we've grown to become the premier creative hub at JTEC.

Our mission is to nurture talent, foster creativity, and provide a platform for students to explore the art of photography and cinematography.
