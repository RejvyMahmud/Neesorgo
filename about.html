Who We Are

NEESORGO is more than just a photography society - we are visual storytellers

From Vision to Reality

Founded in 2018, NEESORGO emerged as the creative heartbeat of Jhenaidah Textile Engineering College. What started as a small group of photography enthusiasts has blossomed into the premier cine-photography society of JTEC.

Our name "NEESORGO" symbolizes our commitment to creating visual narratives that capture the essence of moments, emotions, and stories through the lens.

We believe that every frame has a story to tell, and our mission is to equip students with the technical skills and creative vision to tell those stories compellingly.

Our Purpose

Driven by passion, guided by vision

Our Mission

To nurture creative talent, provide technical education in photography and cinematography, and create a platform where students can express their visual storytelling abilities while building a community of passionate visual artists.

Our Vision

To become the leading student-run photography and filmmaking community in Bangladesh, recognized for innovation, creativity, and technical excellence while empowering the next generation of visual storytellers and content creators.

Our Journey

Milestones that shaped NEESORGO

2018

The Beginning

NEESORGO was founded by a group of passionate photography enthusiasts at JTEC with a vision to create a platform for visual storytelling.

2019

First Exhibition

Organized our first photography exhibition "Frame of Mind" showcasing student work and gaining recognition across campus.

2020

Digital Expansion

Expanded into cinematography and started our YouTube channel, reaching wider audiences with our short films and documentaries.

2022

Award Recognition

Received "Best Student Society" award at JTEC Annual Cultural Festival for our outstanding contributions to campus creativity.

2024

Community Outreach

Launched photography workshops for local schools and community centers, sharing our passion with the wider community.

2025

New Horizons

Expanding into documentary filmmaking and planning our first inter-college photography competition.
