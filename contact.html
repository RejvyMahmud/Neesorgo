Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about NEESORGO

How can I join NEESORGO?

We welcome all JTEC students who are passionate about photography and filmmaking. You can join by filling out our membership form available during orientation events, contacting any executive member, or submitting the contact form on this page. No prior experience is required!

Do I need to own a camera to join?

Not at all! While having a camera is beneficial, it's not mandatory. We have equipment that members can borrow for society activities and events. Many of our workshops also focus on smartphone photography, so you can start with what you have.

What kind of events do you organize?

We host a variety of events including photography workshops, filmmaking competitions, photo exhibitions, guest speaker sessions, photowalks, and collaborative projects. Check our events page for upcoming activities!

Can non-JTEC students participate in your events?

While regular membership is reserved for JTEC students, many of our workshops and exhibitions are open to the public. Follow our social media for announcements about public events and collaborations with other institutions.

How often do you meet?

We have regular meetings every two weeks, plus additional sessions for workshops and project work. The schedule varies each semester based on member availability and ongoing projects.
